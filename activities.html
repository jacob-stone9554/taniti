Activities

Enjoy beach adventures, rainforest hikes, and visits to the active volcano. Try fishing tours,
zip-lining, snorkeling, or relax at local pubs, a microbrewery, and more. With plenty to explore
in the Merriton Landing area, there's something for everyone.

In the city

There's plenty to do without straying too far from the beaten path

In the city, you can:

  • Enjoy local nightlift at pubs, a microbrewery and a new dance club
  • Explore Taniti City and experience the local architecture
  • Visit art galleries showcasing local and international artists
  • Learn about Tanitian culture by visiting the local history museum
  • Take a break and have some fun at the arcade or bowling alley
  • Take a boat or bus tour of the island to see everything Taniti has to offer

Adventure options include:

  • Snorkeling in crystal clear waters
  • Chartered fishing tours for deep sea and sport fishing
  • Exploring hidden beaches away from the city crowds
  • Zip-lining through the lush rainforest canopy
  • Hiking through the rainforest to explore diverse wildlife and scenic trails
  • Take a boat or bus tour of the island to see everything Taniti has to offer
