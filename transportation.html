Transportation

Getting around is easy with a variety of transportation options, including taxis, rental cars, bicycles,
and walkable areas ensure that you can explore the island comfortably and at your own pace

To and from the island

View our options to see what works best for you

Our options include:

  • Most visitors arrive to Taniti by air, flying into the local airport
  • Visitors can arrive at the island on a cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay one night a week
  • We are currently in the process of expanding the airport to support more options
  • The local airport currently supports small jets and propeller planes

Options include:

  • Public buses operate in Taniti City daily from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m.
  • Taxis are available throughout Taniti City for convenient transport
  • Private buses serve destinations outside of Taniti City
  • Rental Cars are offered by a local agency near the airport
  • Bike rentals are available from multiple vendors (helmets are required by law)
  • Taniti City and Merriton Landing are easy to explore on foot
